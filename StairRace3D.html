Stair Race 3D

In Stair Race 3D, you are about to embark on an exciting, fast-paced race up a never-ending staircase. The goal is to climb as high as possible while avoiding obstacles and falling off the stairs. As you move up, the pace increases, and the challenge becomes more intense. Collect power-ups along the way to boost your speed and get past tricky areas. The stairs are never the same, with unpredictable twists, turns, and jumps that require quick reflexes and precise timing to overcome. Will you be able to reach the top and claim victory?
